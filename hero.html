Norwegian Cuisine

Kjøttkaker

Traditional Norwegian meatballs served with potatoes and gravy.

Lutefisk

Dried fish that has been soaked in a lye solution and served with potatoes.

Fårikål

A hearty stew made with mutton and cabbage, considered Norway's national dish.

Smalahove

Smoked sheep's head, traditionally eaten before Christmas.

Gravlaks

Cured salmon with a mixture of sugar, salt, and dill.

About Norway

Norway is a Scandinavian country known for its breathtaking fjords, majestic mountains, and vibrant culture. From the bustling streets of Oslo to the serene beauty of the Arctic Circle, Norway offers unforgettable experiences for every traveler.
