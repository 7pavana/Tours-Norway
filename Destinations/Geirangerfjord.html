Best Time to Visit

The best time to visit Geirangerfjord is from June to August when the weather is mild and the days are long. However, this is also the busiest tourist season. For fewer crowds, consider visiting in May or September.

How to Get There

The nearest airport is Ålesund Airport, Vigra. From there, you can rent a car or take a bus to Geiranger. Alternatively, you can reach Geirangerfjord by ferry from nearby towns like Hellesylt or Ålesund.

Accommodation Options

Geirangerfjord offers a range of accommodation options, from luxury hotels with fjord views to cozy guesthouses and camping sites. Some popular choices include Hotel Union Geiranger, Grande Fjord Hotel, and Geiranger Camping.
